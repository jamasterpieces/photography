At J & A Masterpieces, we believe photography is more than just capturing images—it’s about telling your story. Based in Augusta, GA, our studio specializes in wedding photography, portraits, and event photography that reflect both artistry and authenticity. Whether you’re celebrating love, family, or a once-in-a-lifetime milestone, we approach each session with a focus on creative storytelling. From intimate family portraits to full wedding photography in the CSRA, we create timeless images that you’ll cherish for years to come.

Our Equipment


We believe every memory deserves to be captured with clarity and care. That’s why we invest in professional Canon camera systems, a wide range of lenses, and reliable backup gear.
What this means for you:
Sharp, vibrant images — no matter the lighting or setting.
Confidence on your big day — with multiple camera bodies and dual memory cards, your moments are always backed up.
Prepared for every situation — from intimate portraits to fast-paced wedding receptions, we have the right tools for the job.
We keep our focus on the art of photography, but rest assured, the tools we use are built to deliver quality you can trust.
